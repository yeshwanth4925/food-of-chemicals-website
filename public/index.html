Side Effects / Health Concerns

Chemical Potential Side Effects / Problems
Monosodium Glutamate (E621)Headache, nausea, flushing ("Chinese Restaurant Syndrome")
Aspartame (E951)May trigger headaches; harmful for PKU patients
Sorbic Acid (E200)High doses may cause skin irritation or allergies
Citric Acid (E330)Excess may cause tooth enamel erosion or stomach irritation
Caramel Color IV (E150d)Excess consumption linked to 4-MEI potential cancer risk
Ethyl Vanillin / Artificial FlavorsMay cause allergic reactions, hyperactivity in sensitive children
Fruity flavor compounds (Isoamyl acetate, Ethyl butyrate, etc.)High doses may cause nausea, vomiting, irritation; generally safe in small amounts
BenzaldehydeHigh exposure may irritate eyes, skin, respiratory system
Pectin (E440)Generally safe; very high intake may cause digestive discomfort
